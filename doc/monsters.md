# Monster standards

- All lower-class mages shall have the following stats:
  - HP: 6
  - Evasion: 0%
  - Memory: 5
  - Willpower: 4
  - Normal speed
  - Vision: 5
  - Normal melee%
  - No armor

- All mage creatures (emberlings, sparklings, etc) shall have the following
  stats:
  - HP: 4
  - Evasion: 10%
  - Memory: 3
  - Willpower: 1
  - if fast, then Speed=60
  - Vision: 4
  - Normal melee%
  - Armor: usually zero
